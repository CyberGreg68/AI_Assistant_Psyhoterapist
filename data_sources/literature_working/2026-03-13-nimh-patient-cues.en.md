# Derived patient cues from NIMH anxiety, stress, and child mental health pages

These are operator-authored review cues distilled from the public NIMH sources used in the 2026-03-13 literature batch.
They are intentionally short, patient-side formulations for trigger and phrase review.

- I feel worried all the time and I cannot switch it off.
- I am overwhelmed and I need help calming down right now.
- I cannot sleep because my mind keeps racing.
- I avoid normal things because the anxiety feels too strong.
- I feel tense in my body and I cannot relax.
- I am afraid something bad will happen even when nothing is happening.
- My child seems fearful and worried for weeks and I need help understanding what to do.
- My child talks about wanting to get hurt and I need immediate help.
